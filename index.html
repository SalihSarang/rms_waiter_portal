Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 20, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application RMS Waiter Portal ("Application") created by Salih Sarang (referred to as "we", "us", or "our").

The Application is a Restaurant Management System (RMS) client tool designed specifically for restaurant waiting staff to manage tables, place customer orders, view shift status, and coordinate dining services.

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal & Staff Information

To use this Application, your restaurant administrator assigns you a staff account. We collect:

  • Account Credentials: Email address and password (for secure authentication).
  • Staff Profile: Staff ID, name, role (Waiter), and shift records.

B. App Activity & Operational Data

The Application operates by syncing restaurant actions in real-time. We collect and process:

  • Table & Ordering Records: Table numbers, seated counts, selected food items, order additions, notes, and pricing totals.
  • Alert Notifications: Kitchen ready notifications and server call responses.

2. Device Permissions Required

To provide restaurant operations, the Application requires the following native device permission:

  • INTERNET Access: Required to establish a secure database socket connection with our servers (Firebase Firestore & Firebase Auth) to sync live table and kitchen orders.

We do NOT request or access location trackers, cameras, contact sheets, call history, or native media storage logs.

3. Third-Party Services Used

The Application utilizes standard industry-leading backend services to guarantee data reliability and encryption. These third-party processors maintain their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Authentication: For securely encrypting passwords and verifying logins.
  • Cloud Firestore: For hosting active table lists and kitchen orders.

4. Data Sharing & Third-Party Disclosure

We respect your privacy. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data or your restaurant's business records to marketing brokers or third-party advertisers. Your data is synced solely inside your organization to coordinate order preparations.

5. Security & Encryption

We are committed to safeguarding the confidentiality of your credentials. All network transmissions are strictly encrypted using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL/TLS) connections managed by Google Firebase.

6. Retention and Deletion

Your staff data and operational order logs are retained as long as your account is active in your restaurant's database. If you wish to delete your account or wipe your history, your restaurant administrator can remove your profile directly from the main Firebase console.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is strictly intended for professional waiting staff and business personnel. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend reviewing this page periodically for any changes.
